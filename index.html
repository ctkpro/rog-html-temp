THE SAGA
CONTINUES

G.U.N.

“The only way to win the next world war is to prevent it.” After the great war, under the leadership of Gaian United Nations (G.U.N.), the human race hoped to restore a Golden Age once again.

Their resources were limited, however, and could never be distributed fairly. With the ideal of a “New Order” shining down from the billboards, the real world continued to slowly collapse. In the end, the utopia spoken about so often by the authorities became nothing but an empty promise.

The discontent and disappointment of the common people needed a way out. For a time, the CySports held by the government placated the public, but it could not make up for the false hope in reality. Before the crowd’s rage roared toward those in authority, the frenzy was redirected to those who held extraordinary powers, the G.A.M.E.R.s, who carried the original sin of the great war.

G.A.M.E.Rs

A long time ago, a special group of people appeared with unique gifts, enabling them to resonate with the element silicon. These special abilities have attracted jealousy and persecution from society rather than blessings. Suppressing their gifts, G.A.M.E.R.s have hidden themselves throughout history. In secret, they use the “Dauntless Eyes” as a totem to advance human civilization in these digital times.

In a worldwide trade war, G.A.M.E.R.s were exposed to human eyes by accident. People began envious, but before long became terrified of those talents. Presumably, G.A.M.E.R.s are supposed to promote and develop civilization; in truth, they have been held captive by the authorities, fighting in a meaningless chaos and slowly withering away.

FATE WAR

One G.A.M.E.R., who dreamt of equal rights for his brethren fell victim to a secret, inhumane experiment by a syndicate. His family was wiped out in a similar conspiracy. A broken dream and hatred toward human beings turned him into a fallen angel called FATE. He swore to eradicate the human race from Earth as revenge. Wherever his armies go, despair and grief follow.

As the FATE War brought the human race to the brink of extinction, a secret society of G.A.M.E.R.s was revealed to be standing against FATE’s invasion. Working with G.U.N., this secret association planned a raid against FATE’s forces, initiating a frontal conflict. When the crisis was resolved, the new world era was named the Calendarium Gaian.

Over the past seven years, the safety net governed by G.U.N. was supposed to maintain world peace, but has become a surveillance net to monitor and persecute people that the authorities disagreed with. Any movement, no matter how small it is, could trigger repercussions from those in power.

The G.A.M.E.R.s who once saved the world are being hunted down by this network. They are tortured and charged with trumped-up accusations…

FOR THOSE WHO DARE

A sweeping destruction is imminent. The population of G.A.M.E.R.s is under threat. Retreating will lead to an end that is even worse than death. Seven heroes stand out as an aegis against that future.

AKIRA is a battle-seasoned rōnin, SE7EN is an assassin known for her agility and cold beauty, HORSEM4N is the Reaper who longs for salvation, GO is an armored giant, AchT is a hacker passing between the physical and digital dimensions, OMNI is an AI built with black technology, and PKD is a lone wolf who can control micro-bots with his psychokinesis.

Gathering at the crossroads of fate, the champions are ready to reignite their souls. Their creed:
We shall break all rules and awaken our heroic spirits with bravery.
We shall be free from bonds and be reborn in the fire of our belief.
We shall build the ark to conquer our fate with iron and blood, sailing fearlessly toward the land of liberation and promise.
