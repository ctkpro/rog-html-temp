SE7EN

“I FEEL MY FATE IN WHAT I CANNOT FEAR.”

SE7EN’s real name is Zayina Steinberger. Due to a severe accident during her childhood, Zayina lost most of her body. But due to a stroke of luck, the legendary cyborg craftsman Kitō Takayuki granted her a second life with a surgery joining her consciousness to his combat cyborg No. 7. From that day forward, she was known only as SE7EN. In her new life, SE7EN received extreme training from her uncle AKIRA, and has become an assassin serving for the secret society. Cold and silent, SE7EN’s exquisite face doesn’t show any specific racial features. With the mystery and aesthetics that come with the cyborg, SE7EN was scouted to be a model on the catwalk. Her most distinguishing feature is the biochemical hannya tattoo on her back. Furthermore, she has seven special abilities, such as Damascus steel knife, cybernetic chain-whip, active camouflage, bullet time accelerator, and more yet unrevealed…
